About Us

×

We bring authentic Mexican cuisine with our very own signature touch. You will not want to miss out on our mouthwatering Burritos and Chilaquiles. We are on Kingston Road, Toronto.

Our chefs are from Mexico, and they are masters of their craft. They ensure every dish on the plate tastes delicious and looks appetizing. We are strict on hygiene and certified by food authorities and health officials. We are delighting people of all categories.

***************************

El Churrito was founded in 1960 by Late Mr. Pablo Ignacio Jeronimo in Manitoba Colony. Later, when the founder migrated to Toronto. He started a small restaurant in 1975 that could serve 5-10 customers at a time. The founder's goal was to bring Authenticity of Mexican cuisine at affordable prices to people of all categories.

Miss. Loretta Ignacio Jeronimo, currently owning and running her three-generations old family business. Our quality of food and service remains the same for ages. Rich flavors, Fresh ingredients, Years of experience are put on your plates as dishes to delight your tastebuds. You will have serval WOW moments with our dishes.
